What is Fauna?

Fauna was formed in 1996 in the city of Jankoy, located within the Crimean region in Ukraine. The organization was formed to improve public awareness and participation in both sociological and environmental problems in the Jankoy region. Fauna also works to promote the general public, (with a special focus towards children) to live a healthly wholesome lifestyle, and to be respectful towards nature and protect nature for the future.

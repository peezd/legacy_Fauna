Emblem

The Circle - a symbol unity and unifying natures and person, unity and love to nature, pure highcomoral dreams and noble deals.
In the centre, the bird with raised wing protecting the globe, protecting it from ruinous mismanagement activity of the person. Importance of the waterfowl - a join different poetry to lifes, in water, on the sky, on the land.
The Bird - a symbol of the liberty, liberties of the choice of the life way of the person (the life or death, salvation or doom).
The White colour of the bird - personifies the purity showers and dreams.

2001 Work Report

Page 1

1.   A project was undertaken during winter time with the goal of protecting the habitats of winter birds, and to accomplish this goal the following activities were undertaken;:
- Organized a meeting with activists of the ecological organization, “Ekologiya I Mir” and organization DOEO FAUNA , with 35 total participants:
- Produced and distributed leaflets;
- Built feeding troughs;
- Inspected habitat areas in Northern Crimea near Lake Sivash; organized questioning residents of the Jankoy region about undertaking the action;

- Prepared two television broadcasts; The first about wintering birds in Crimea and the action in process with the goal of preserving the birds. The second about water-marsh land, and the action undertaken towards saving water-marsh lands.

(Adzhieva L., Tuvysheva Anna, Tuvysheva Sasha., Medzhitova Elya, Useinova Lilya, Useinov Remzi, Dementieva Lena, Dementiev Misha, Bachuk Irbut, Kulinich Nastia, Lugovskaya Olya, Tuvysheva T and Tuvysheva L.V.

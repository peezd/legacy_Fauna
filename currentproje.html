"Donesti do Cerdtsa Kazhdovo". (Carry to each heart)

 

Information on project "Donesti do Cerdtsa Kazhdovo".

(Carry to each heart)

 

This new project begins in August by Public Childrens' Ecological Organization "Fauna" with the name "Carry to each heart", formed within the framework of the program "Way to the future", which is executed by ISAR Ukrainian organization ISAR "Ednannya", budgeted by American Fund CH.S. Motta.

In the Jankoy region there are many ecological problems. The cutting down of woodland trees is an especially large scale problem that needs to be addressed. Public attention of the rural inhabitants needs to be called to these problems. Through this project different actions are organized that lead to increased ecological and legal information distribution.

There will be a seminar for leaders of the rural ecological groups, which will herein after work with rural youth, on preparation and undertaking nature conservation actions and campaigns. Actions will be undertaken on October 22 (Holiday of the Evergreen Bush), 22 March (World Water Day), 22 April (Worldwide Day of the Land). Informational material will be distributed and posted on web-sites on the Internet. All are their own actions we shall illuminate in СМИ. Performing the project will be active members of our organization; Tuvysheva L.V., Dementeva N.A., Bagatyrly E., Dementieva E., Evans M., Tuvysheva А.

The Project is calculated for a duration of 11 months. The project will be functioning in close co-operation with public bodies and state organizations. СМИ will assist the further development of the ecological public motion in our city and region.
